Joonhee Lim

I am a Ph.D. candidate in Robotics at KAIST, working in the Decision & Motion Planning Team at AXE Lab, advised by Prof. Dongsuk Kum.

My research focuses on decision-making and motion planning for autonomous vehicles, with a particular interest in collision avoidance, reinforcement learning, and E2E autonomous driving.
